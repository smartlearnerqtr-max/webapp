THIET KE DATABASE DE XUAT

Du an: Ban hoc thong minh
Huong trien khai: Webapp/PWA cho desktop va Android
De xuat CSDL: PostgreSQL
Ly do: de mo rong tot, ho tro quan he ro rang, truy van bao cao on, co the dung JSON cho cau hinh hoat dong khi can.

I. NGUYEN TAC THIET KE
- Don vi giao cho hoc sinh la bai hoc.
- Moi bai hoc gom nhieu hoat dong.
- He thong co 3 vai tro nguoi dung: giao vien, phu huynh, hoc sinh.
- Hoc sinh co the tham gia nhieu lop.
- Phu huynh co the lien ket voi nhieu hoc sinh.
- Tien do can luu duoc theo bai hoc, theo hoat dong, theo mon hoc, theo lop va theo thoi gian.
- He thong chi goi y nang do kho, khong tu dong nang muc.
- Gemini API key do nguoi dung nhap trong phan cai dat, nhung nen duoc luu va su dung an toan o backend.

II. CAC BANG COT LOI

Muc dich:
- Bang tai khoan dang nhap chung cho ca giao vien, phu huynh, hoc sinh.
Cot chinh:
- id
- email
- phone
- password_hash
- role: teacher | parent | student
- status: active | invited | inactive
- last_login_at
- created_at
- updated_at
Ghi chu:
- Email va phone nen cho phep null, nhung it nhat mot trong hai phai co neu dung de dang nhap/moi tai khoan.
